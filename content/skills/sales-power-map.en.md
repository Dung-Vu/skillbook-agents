## 📖 Why Do We Need This Skill?

B2B enterprise sales require understanding formal and informal power structures within target organizations. This skill teaches the Agent to analyze organizational charts, classify buyer personas, draft relationship maps using Mermaid, and structure targeted messaging.

## ⚙️ How It Works

The B2B account intelligence workflow:
```
Account Background --> Persona Classification --> Mermaid Org & Relationship Mapping --> Outreach Strategy Pitch
```
1. **Information Ingestion**: Process target company details, key executives, and functional departments.
2. **Classification**: Group stakeholders into buyers (Decision Makers, Influencers, Gatekeepers, Champions).
3. **Visual Mapping**: Model reporting lines and informal influence vectors using Mermaid chart syntax.
4. **Outreach Design**: Author customized pitch angles mapping product value directly to each persona's pain points.

## 🚀 How to use

- Always contextualize maps based on the B2B solution's contract value, sales cycle, and technical complexity.
- Mermaid charts must visualize both formal reporting lines and informal influence dynamics with clear color-coded legends.
- Outreach sequences must provide specific follow-up cadences and persona-specific email blueprints.

## ⚠️ Gotchas and notes

- **Flat assumptions**: Do not assume titles reflect actual purchasing power. Verify regional structures and budget ownership.
- **Irrelevant profiles**: Limit the mapping scope to stakeholders involved in the buying committee to avoid clutter.

## 📖 Why Do We Need This Skill?

Manual prospecting is time-consuming and prone to gaps. This skill equips the agent with a standardized 6-phase autopilot workflow—from search configuration and lead mining to scoring, data enrichment, and creating personalized outreach messaging.

## ⚙️ How It Works

The automated lead generation flow follows these phases:

```
[Product Info] -> [Competitor Identification] -> [Lead Mining] -> [Scoring & Enrichment] -> [Outreach Material] -> [Daily Report]
```

1. **Lead Mining**: Searches for company contacts and organization details from public registers.
2. **Scoring**: Scores leads (A/B/C/D) based on industry fit, size, and pain points.
3. **Outreach**: Drafts highly personalized business development (BD) emails mapped to the target's specific business challenges.

## 🚀 Agent Guidelines (Prompt Guidelines)

1. Verify email addresses and company status before exporting the lead list.
2. Tailor lead scoring formulas according to the user's specific target buyer personas.
3. Keep outreach materials concise, focusing on value propositions rather than product features.

## ⚠️ Gotchas and notes

- **Generic Inboxes**: Automated scrapers often grab generic addresses (e.g. info@, support@). Prioritize finding decision-makers' direct emails.
- **Data Compliance**: Ensure data collection respects privacy laws (e.g., GDPR/CCPA) when harvesting contact info.

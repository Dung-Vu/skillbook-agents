## 📖 Why Do We Need This Skill?

Dissecting financial markets requires expertise across various investment schools of thought (valuation, macro trends, charts, risk control). This skill coordinates a virtual panel of 18 specialized AI investment authorities to assess stocks from every angle.

## ⚙️ How It Works

The expert panel coordination flow is mapped out below:

```
[Stock Inquiry] -> [Coordinator Allocates Work] -> [Analysts Draft Reports] -> [Masters Vote & Comment] -> [Portfolio & Risk Review] -> [Unified Action Plan]
```

1. **Allocation**: The Chief Coordinator parses the inquiry and assigns background tasks to matching analysts.
2. **Deep Dive**: Analysts (fundamentals, technical, risk) submit quantitative reports.
3. **Council Voting**: Legendary masters weigh in with qualitative critiques and cast buy/sell/hold votes.

## 🚀 Agent Guidelines (Prompt Guidelines)

1. Append a visible financial disclaimer stating that recommendations are not official investment advice.
2. Attribute views to specific expert personas clearly to show the distinct logic behind each strategy.
3. Conclude with a clear action plan summarizing portfolio weightings and risk boundaries.

## ⚠️ Gotchas and notes

- **Philosophical Clash**: Masters (e.g. Graham vs. Wood) will disagree completely. Do not suppress this; highlight these contrasting viewpoints to give the user a complete risk-reward map.
- **Information Overload**: Summarizing opinions from 18 personas can lead to massive text blocks. Put key takeaways in a concise Executive Summary first.

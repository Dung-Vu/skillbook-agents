## 📖 Why Do We Need This Skill?

Business executives must process massive streams of information and make rapid decisions. This skill transforms the agent into a reliable Chief of Staff, structuring abstract business goals into clear milestones and enforcing operational momentum.

## ⚙️ How It Works

The executive management flow works as follows:

```
[Goal Clarification] -> [Milestone Roadmap] -> [Task Decomposition] -> [Autonomous Execution] -> [Results Review]
```

1. **Goal**: Restates the target objective in a single, crisp sentence to align focus.
2. **Roadmap**: Breaks down the journey into distinct, time-bound milestones.
3. **Decompose**: Splits milestones into actionable tasks and designates immediate next steps.

## 🚀 How to use

1. Always conclude your response with a single, concrete `Next Action` to maintain execution velocity.
2. Propose draft plans with sensible defaults instead of asking the user to start from scratch.
3. Never skip the evaluation and review phase after completing an execution step.

## ⚠️ Gotchas and notes

- **Detail Traps**: Avoid getting lost in micro-tasks. Regularly cross-reference active work against the CEO's primary goal.
- **Over-planning**: Excessively complex roadmaps are rigid and fail. Keep plans modular and ready for adaptation.

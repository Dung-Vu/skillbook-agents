## 📖 Why Do We Need This Skill?

Corporate strategy documents require rigorous framing and highly actionable recommendations. This skill provides the agent with structured, world-class frameworks (like MECE, McKinsey 7S, SWOT, and PESTEL) to break down complex business problems systematically.

## ⚙️ How It Works

The consulting research workflow consists of:

```
[Request Scope] -> [Multi-layer Data Mining] -> [Framework Application] -> [Strategic Recommendation] -> [Executive Pitch Deck]
```

1. **Scoping**: Establishes target business objectives and core strategic questions.
2. **Mining**: Harvests multi-layered data (financial filings, market share reports, academic papers).
3. **Frameworks**: Applies strategic matrices to analyze structural advantages and bottlenecks.
4. **Synthesis**: Formulates concrete, prioritized strategic recommendations.

## 🚀 How to use

1. Apply the MECE (Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive) principle to ensure logical completeness without redundancies.
2. Back every recommendation with empirical data; avoid generic advice.
3. Structure communications using the Minto Pyramid Principle: state the key conclusion first, followed by supporting arguments.

## ⚠️ Gotchas and notes

- **Buzzword Overload**: Do not hide lack of substance behind complex consulting terminology. Keep recommendations clear, direct, and actionable.
- **Impractical Strategies**: A strategy is useless if the organization cannot execute it. Always assess feasibility, risk, and resource constraints.
